Hi! I'm Jodi,

A front-end web developer from Fort Collins, Colorado with a love for creating visually rich and aesthetic experiences for users.

First impressions are a real thing, especially for a website. I love the nitty-gritty details of code that go into making a website stand out to clients and users.

When I'm not coding and drinking 4 cups of coffee a day, you can find me doing anything creative, enjoying the great outdoors, taking up a few photography gigs, or exploring the world with my fiancé.

I hold a strong work ethic, full belief in wholehearted effort, and a willingness to complete whatever task is placed ahead of me; I am known as a determined creative, and strong team player, with an abundance of optimism.
